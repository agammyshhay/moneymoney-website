How it works?

Two ways to get started — pick whatever suits you.

No install

Web App

Import files from every bank and credit card in Israel straight through your browser — download the file from your bank's website and drag it into the app. You can also enter transactions manually and manage your whole budget from any device, with no installation.

  • Import files from banks and credit-card companies (Excel / CSV / OFX)
  • Enter transactions manually
  • Works in any browser — computer, phone or tablet
Open the app
or
Open source

Automatic sync

Install the software once, connect your banks — and transactions sync to the app automatically in the background, with no manual file downloads.

  • Automatic sync from all banks and credit cards
  • Passwords are stored encrypted on your computer only
  • Runs in the background — no need to remember to update
Download for desktop
You can start with the web app alone and add the desktop software later, at any stage.

Security & Privacy

Passwords never leave your computer. Your details stay yours — and only yours.

Passwords stay with you

Your bank usernames and passwords are encrypted on your computer only. They are never sent to a server, to the cloud, or anywhere else.

Open source — check for yourself

Every line of code is open on GitHub. You can verify exactly what happens with your data.

Accessible from any device

Only the transactions move to the app — no passwords, no login details. Sign in from any device and see everything up to date.
